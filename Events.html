Bouldering for beginners

Every Tuesday evening from 7:30 pm to 8:30pm you have the chance to join a group of people in their bouldering activities. You don't have to sign up before, there are lot's of dutchies but also internationals.

Where? Kerketuinenweg 55, 2544 CV Den Haag

When? Every Tuesday 7:30pm  - 8:30pm

Prize: 7€

Eat cake and learn Dutch

In a language cafe you can have a nice chit chat with locals, practice your language skills, play games, mingle with all different kinds of people and of course have a coffe, tea or cake. You have to sign up yourself beforehand via a form on their website.

Where? Savoiepad 14,5627 DX Eindhoven

When? Every Wednesday 10:30am - 12.00am

Prize: Entree for free

Pub Quiz evening 

You want to prove your general knowledge and get in touch with locals and internationals? Then this pub quiz about general knowledge is a perfect match! You don't need to know a lot about the Netherlands to have a good score. No sign up needed.
