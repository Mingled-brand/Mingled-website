MINGLE-MAKER

Because people make you feel home!  

We match YOU to your fitting community in the Netherlands, easy, fast and without fees!   

How does it work?

You will answer questions in our questionaire about your personal interests, hobbies, characteristics and demographics. We are in touch with several communities all over the place in the Netherlands, who also gave us their values, interests and hobbies. According to that, we will match you to your most fitting group.

To participate in the survey, please give us your contact details!

We will come back to you as soon as possible!

 

Example questions

  • What are you usually doing to relax?
  • How do you like to spend your Friday evening?
  • Are you an introvert or extrovert, or somewhere in the middle?
