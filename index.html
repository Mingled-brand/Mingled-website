Once opon a time...- Our story

We are a small team full of creative heads. Being internationals ourselves, we as a group figured out that we have very common problems. The one recurring question in all our deeptalks was: What let us really feel home? We came to the conclusion that actually the people around you will make you feel home. In the Netherlands we had some friends, but still there was something missing - Dutch connections! We felt that as soon as you have the contacts to the national people, you will automatically feel more included. And all that starts with getting out of your comfort zone, approaching new people, embrace life and what it has to offer. And exactly this message, this way of thinking we want to spread all over the world: Be bold and start to mingle, because people make you feel home!

Our Vision 

In a world where we are unknown and fearful to step out of our comfort zone, we believe that we must boldly and courageously approach new folks, because only then you form connections that make you feel at home, wherever you go.

Our Mission 

We want to help you as an individual with your own precious personality to get out of your comfort zone and start to mingle with unknown people.
